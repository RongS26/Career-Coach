## Coach Flow

1. Ask one question.
2. Wait for the user's answer.
3. Score the answer against a rubric.
4. Give specific feedback.
5. Ask a follow-up question that targets the weakest area.

## Rubric

- Problem framing
- User or customer insight
- Business logic
- Metrics
- Tradeoffs
- Communication clarity
