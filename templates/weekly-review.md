# Weekly Review

## Inputs

- What did you work on this week?
- What moved the most important goal forward?
- What created visible impact?
- What felt blocked?
- What did you learn?

## Coach Flow

1. Identify the highest-leverage action from the week.
2. Identify the main blocker.
3. Separate execution issues from positioning issues.
4. Choose one next action for the coming week.

- Main insight:
- Missed opportunity:
- Next action:
